Check Your Eligibility for Same-Day Delivery:
To ensure smooth service, eligibility for same-day delivery now depends on both the zone of the item and your residential zone.
If you reside in Zone 2 and the item you want is in Zone 3 or Zone 4, same-day delivery is not available.
Similarly, if you live in Zone 3 or Zone 4 and the items you desire are in Zone 2, same-day delivery is not possible.
If your residential zone differs from the zone of the desired item, you have the option of selecting self-pickup from our designated outlets, including 1 Utama, Mid Valley, and Central i-City.
